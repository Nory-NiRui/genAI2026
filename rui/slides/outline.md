## Detailed Speaking Notes

### **Speaker 1: Problem & Solution (1.5 minutes)**
*Project Lead - NI Rui*

**Opening Hook (10 seconds)**
- "Where should I rent? Who should I rent to? These are critical questions Hong Kong retailers face every day."

**Problem Statement (30 seconds)**
- Hong Kong retail market is highly competitive and costly
- Small and medium-sized retailers struggle to find suitable locations
- Current approach: relying on personal experience, real estate agents, or limited public data
- Result: poor site selection → high fixed costs → business failure

**Solution Introduction (30 seconds)**
- Introduce SiteWise: a data-driven location recommendation platform
- Target users: SMEs in Hong Kong retail sector
- Key value proposition: Data-driven recommendations tailored to specific business needs
- Transition: "But how does it actually work?"

**Design Rationale**
- Addresses a real, urgent need in Hong Kong's competitive retail market
- Makes enterprise-grade location analysis affordable for SMEs
- User-centered approach: customizable parameters based on individual business needs

---

### **Speaker 2: Technology & How It Works (1 minute)**
*Data Analytics Lead - QU Yichen*

**How the Platform Works (50 seconds)**
1. **Input Stage**: Users provide customizable parameters
   - Business type
   - Target customer profile
   - Rental budget
   - Preferred areas

2. **Processing Stage**: Four-step analysis
   - Data Collection: Aggregate multi-source data (transportation, demographics, rental rates)
   - Data Processing: Clean and structure the data
   - Modeling: Compute site selection scores by weighting key factors
     - Customer fit
     - Accessibility
     - Rental costs
     - Other critical factors

3. **Output Stage**: Clear, actionable results
   - Top 5 recommended addresses
   - Detailed analysis report
   - Visualized data for easy interpretation

**Key Insight (10 seconds)**
- "We transform complex data into simple, actionable insights through proven modeling techniques"

## Questions & Issues for Further Discussion

### Technical Questions
1. **Data Accuracy**: How frequently is data updated? What's the latency between data collection and recommendation?
2. **Model Validation**: What's the historical accuracy rate of location recommendations? Do we have case studies or pilot data?
3. **Scalability**: How will the platform handle high traffic volumes during peak usage periods?
4. **Data Privacy**: How do we handle user data and maintain privacy compliance with Hong Kong regulations?

### Business Questions
5. **Monetization**: What's the revenue model? (Freemium? Subscription? B2B partnerships?)
6. **User Acquisition**: How will we reach and onboard SME retailers?
7. **Real Estate Platform Integration**: Have we approached major platforms? What's the timeline?
8. **Competitive Response**: How will major real estate players react? Could they block our access to data?

### Product & UX Questions
9. **Mobile Experience**: Is there a mobile app, or just web-based?
10. **Recommendation Validation**: How do users verify recommendations match their actual experience?
11. **Update Frequency**: How often do users need to re-query? Can they save preferences?

### Market & Growth Questions
12. **Geographic Expansion**: Which cities should we prioritize after Hong Kong? (Shanghai? Singapore? Southeast Asia?)
13. **Partnership Strategy**: Should we partner with business incubators, SME associations, or industry bodies?
14. **Regulatory Risks**: Are there any regulatory barriers in Hong Kong or other markets?

### Operational Questions
15. **Team Capacity**: Can the current 4-person team handle product development, marketing, and operations?
16. **Funding**: What are our capital requirements for development, data infrastructure, and user acquisition?

## Design & Rationale Summary

### Core Design Principles
1. **Simplicity**: Reduce complexity of site selection into simple, intuitive interface
2. **Data-Driven**: Replace intuition with objective analysis
3. **Affordability**: Make enterprise analytics accessible to SMEs
4. **Objectivity**: Remove bias from real estate agent recommendations

### Problem-Solution Fit
- **Problem**: SME retailers lack access to affordable, comprehensive site selection tools
- **Solution**: Automated platform aggregating public data into actionable recommendations
- **Why Now**: Hong Kong's data infrastructure and open data platforms enable this

### Competitive Positioning
- **Not a Real Estate Agency**: We're a decision-support tool, not a property broker
- **Not an Expensive Enterprise Tool**: We democratize site selection analytics
- **Better Than DIY**: Saves retailers months of research and analysis work

### User Value Proposition
- Reduce time spent on site selection (days → minutes)
- Reduce risk of poor location choice
- Reduce overall business setup costs
- Increase likelihood of business success
